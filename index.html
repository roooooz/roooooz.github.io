About Me

I am a Senior Applied Research Scientist in Cruise LLC , working on AV perception. I obtained my Ph.D. from UC Irvine where I was advised by Prof. Charless Fowlkes. I also spent wonderful time at Amazon Science, Adobe Research, Multimedia Lab and Multimedia Research Center as a research intern. My research interest lies in human action/pose/ shape understanding, and how to leverage scene context/long-term temporal modeling/short-term motion as an assistant. I maintain awesome-human-pose-estimation with focus on 3d human pose estimation.

Open positions: Cruise is hiring for multiple positions (intern and full time), send me an email if you are interested.
